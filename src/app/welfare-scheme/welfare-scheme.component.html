Welfare Schemes

The lifestyle of an independant woman is determined by the freedom of staying anywhere the woman wants to or wishes to. For this reason, enhancing this possibility WE has brought an offer of providing accomodation to our empowering women. We have the facility of providing accomodation in the major metropolitan cities and even the sub-urban and the rural areas. The women of our society should feel comfortable at all places, hence this venture of ours will provide a sense of strength, liberty and financial stability along with reliability to our empowering women.
